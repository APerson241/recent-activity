Recently Active Users

Tools > Recently Active Users

This tool searches for experienced editors that have recently edited.* It's an attempt at a replacement for WP:HAU. Please note that many of these editors may be quite busy; try to only contact those editors that seem to be replying to people on their talk pages.

*"Experienced" means 10,000 edits or more; "recently" means that an editor appears on Special:RecentChanges - i.e. they edited less than 5-10 minutes ago.
