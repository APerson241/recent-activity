Recently Active Users

Tools > Recently Active Users

This tool searches for experienced editors who have recently edited.* Recent activity does not imply that these editors are available to assist immediately, so first view their recent contributions to determine whether you would be interrupting. In case your contact is not available, we recommend filing administrative requests through the normal noticeboards rather than contacting admins directly.

*In specific, this tool searches editors who have edited in the last ten minutes and have made over 10,000 edits in total. This tool replaces Wikipedia:Highly Active Users.
